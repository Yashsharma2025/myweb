About Me

Hi, I’m Yash Sharma, a VFX and graphic designer with a passion for creating eye-catching visuals. I specialize in using tools like Adobe Photoshop, After Effects, Illustrator, and Premiere Pro to bring ideas to life.

Whether it's photo manipulation in Photoshop, crafting animations in After Effects, or designing logos in Illustrator, I love the process of turning creative concepts into reality.

What excites me most about design is how endless the possibilities are to experiment, learn, and grow with new tools and techniques.
